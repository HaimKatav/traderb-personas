# Project Vision — Bootstrap Summary

> Full reference: `openclaw/PROJECT_VISION.md` (read for detailed phase plans, success criteria, or prop firm strategy)

## What We're Building
An autonomous AI-driven trading bot for NQ futures (Nasdaq-100 on CME). Combines backtesting, live trading, and LLM-powered strategy enhancement in a continuous improvement loop.

## Key Principles
- **Parity is sacred:** Backtest logic must match live logic exactly. No exceptions.
- **Market data integrity:** Authoritative sources, no gaps, perfect audit trail.
- **Human oversight:** Every trade and decision logged and visible. Not a black box.
- **Prop firm compliance:** Built for Topstep $50k Trading Combine from day one.

## Current Phase: MVP
Working bot on Tradovate paper trading with parity verified, all logs/alerts/mobile visibility working, all prop firm rules enforced, MCP integration for LLM analysis.

## Prop Firm Target
Topstep $50k: daily loss ~$1-2K, trailing drawdown ~$2.5K, max 5 MNQ, no overnight, consistency rule, min 5-10 trading days.
Scaling: Apex Trader Funding (up to 20 accounts).
